# Week 7 AI Audit Exercise

## Lab Overview
In this lab, participants will engage in the process of auditing AI systems in a simulated environment, focusing on diagnostic tools within the MedTech space.

## Scenario (MedTech Solutions DiagnosticAI)
Participants will work with a fictional company, MedTech Solutions, which has developed an AI tool named DiagnosticAI. The tool aids healthcare providers in making diagnostic decisions.

## Activity 1: Audit Planning
- Define the scope of the audit.
- Identify stakeholders and their roles. 
- Establish audit objectives and key performance indicators.

## Activity 2: Evidence Collection
Participants will collect evidence through the following three packages:
1. **Package 1: Documentation Review**  
   - Review of system documentation, user manuals, and compliance reports.

2. **Package 2: Interviews**  
   - Conduct interviews with stakeholders including developers, healthcare providers, and patients.

3. **Package 3: System Testing**  
   - Perform functional testing on the AI tool to assess its performance against established benchmarks.

## Activity 3: Finding Development
- Analyze collected evidence.
- Document findings related to compliance, performance, and ethical considerations.

## Activity 4: Audit Report Summary
- Prepare a summary report highlighting key findings and recommendations.
- Include visuals to support findings.

## Lab Deliverables
1. Complete audit report.
2. Presentation summarizing audit findings.
3. Documentation of evidence collection activities.

## Evaluation Rubric
- Clarity of audit report
- Quality of evidence collected
- Depth of analysis and findings
- Presentation skills

## Additional Resources
- Articles on AI ethics in healthcare.
- Guidelines on auditing AI systems.

## Tips for Success
- Collaborate effectively with your team members.
- Stay organized and document your processes thoroughly.

## Bonus Challenge
- Propose improvements to the DiagnosticAI system based on the findings from your audit.
