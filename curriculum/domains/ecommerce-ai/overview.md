# E-commerce AI Specialist Certification

## Overview
This certification covers essential aspects of AI in e-commerce, including personalization, recommendation engines, dynamic pricing, and fraud prevention.

## Modules

- **Module 01:** E-commerce AI Landscape
- **Module 02:** Recommendation Systems
- **Module 03:** Personalization & Customer Experience
- **Module 04:** Dynamic Pricing & Promotion
- **Module 05:** Visual Search & Product Discovery
- **Module 06:** Conversational Commerce
- **Module 07:** Fraud Detection & Prevention
- **Module 08:** Inventory & Demand Forecasting
- **Module 09:** Customer Analytics & Segmentation
- **Module 10:** Implementation

- **Final Project:** Develop an AI strategy for a retailer with $500M annual GMV that incorporates the learned concepts. 

### Examples from Industry
- **Shopify:** Leveraging AI for personalized shopping experiences.
- **Salesforce Commerce Cloud:** AI-driven recommendations and customer insights. 
- **Adobe Commerce:** Using AI for marketing automation and analytics.
