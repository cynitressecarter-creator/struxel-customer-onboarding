# Module 02: AI in Banking and Lending

## Overview
This module explores how artificial intelligence is revolutionizing banking and lending, focusing on three key areas: credit risk assessment, anti-money laundering (AML), and loan underwriting.

## Topics Covered
1. **Credit Risk**  
   - Use of AI in assessing borrower risk  
   - Case Study: JPMorgan's use of machine learning to evaluate credit scores.

2. **Anti-Money Laundering**  
   - AI techniques for detecting suspicious transactions  
   - Example: Goldman Sachs's AI system for AML monitoring.

3. **Loan Underwriting**  
   - AI-driven decision-making processes in underwriting  
   - Real-world application: Upstart's automated underwriting system.

## Conclusion
The integration of AI into these areas not only enhances efficiency but also helps in maintaining regulatory compliance and safeguarding against risks.
